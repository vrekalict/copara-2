# Product — Copara

## Register

**Marketing site:** calm Canadian family-tech positioning for separated parents and professionals.  
**Product app:** mobile PWA at `/app` — same trust principles, denser UI.

## Users

**Primary:** Separated or divorcing co-parents in Canada (English and French), often under court-adjacent stress. They use Copara on mobile (PWA) during custody exchanges, scheduling conflicts, and expense disputes.

**Secondary:** Mediators, family lawyers, and parenting coordinators who observe circles where permitted, generate summaries, and export organized records.

**Tertiary:** Third-party caregivers with scoped visibility only.

## Product Purpose

Copara helps separated parents communicate neutrally, coordinate custody schedules, track shared expenses, and keep **tamper-evident records suitable for review by legal professionals** — without in-app money movement or app-store install friction (PWA-first).

## Brand Personality

**Three words:** Calm · Trustworthy · Clear

Voice is plain, warm, and serious — never dramatic, fear-based, cute, or overly legalistic. The product should feel like a reliable record-keeper, not a therapist or adversary.

## Marketing Anti-patterns (do not use)

- Generic SaaS landing templates, lorem ipsum, vague copy
- Fake testimonials, logos, screenshots, or app-store badges
- Purple AI gradients, sparkles, 3D blobs, cheesy stock family photos
- Fear-based conflict marketing or “court-approved” overclaims
- Aggressive red UI or childish illustration styles

## Trust & Legal Sensitivity

- Never claim certified, court-approved, court-admissible, or legal advice
- Use: “Tamper-evident records suitable for review by legal professionals”
- Privacy Policy and Terms are **drafts** — must be reviewed by a lawyer before launch
- Canadian-focused pricing in CAD; bilingual en/fr

| `/early-access` | Capture interest; honest about backend TODO |
| `/fr` | Real French landing (not placeholder) |

## Design Principles (product + marketing)

1. **De-escalate by default** — copy and color lower temperature
2. **Trust through transparency** — timestamps, verification, disclaimers
3. **Mobile-first** — 44px tap targets, excellent responsive layout
4. **Bilingual parity** — en/fr as first-class
5. **Records over rhetoric** — integrity and professional access over social features
