# Impeccable — Copara

Design context for AI-assisted UI work on this project.

## Design Context (pointer)

Full strategic context: **[PRODUCT.md](./PRODUCT.md)**  
Visual system tokens and rules: **[DESIGN.md](./DESIGN.md)**

**Register:** marketing (public site at `/`, `/pricing`, `/features/*`, etc.) + product (PWA at `/app`)

**Quick principles:** Calm · Trustworthy · Clear — de-escalate by default, no legal overclaims, Canadian-focused, bilingual parity.

**Stack:** Next.js 16 + Tailwind v4 + shadcn base-nova + Geist. Marketing primary `#2563eb`.

**Anti-patterns:** Generic SaaS templates, lorem ipsum, fake testimonials, purple AI gradients, fear-based copy, court-certification claims.

**Marketing audit checklist:**
- Homepage explains Copara within 5 seconds
- All pages have unique SEO metadata + JSON-LD where required
- Pricing in CAD with monthly/yearly toggle
- Legal pages include prominent draft-for-lawyer-review notice
- Mockups match real app UI patterns
- 44px minimum tap targets on mobile nav/CTAs

**Bolder pass (Impeccable):** Stronger hierarchy via `.display` / `.lead` / `.eyebrow`, accent-rule hero, trust pills, deeper slate, larger section rhythm — no gradients or AI slop.
